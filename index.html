About Us

What About Us?

Diaho.Craft is a business making artificial flower arrangements. We provide flower bouquet making services using artificial paper flowers, money bouquets, and custom flower bouquets such as dolls, snacks, or others. We also provide handicrafts other than flower bouquets such as parcels, wedding gifts, and others.

Diaho.Craft was first founded in 2018 starting from making paper flower bouquets which were used as personal gifts. Then we started receiving requests for making paper flower bouquets, which then grew to receiving requests for making money flower bouquets until now we accept making costume flower bouquets or making other craft products.
